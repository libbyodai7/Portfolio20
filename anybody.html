Is Anybody There?





Is Anybody There? - Stellar Quines Theatre Company - Make Do and Mend Residency - March 2021 - present

Is Anybody There? is an interactive performance work in development; exploring what it means to connect in a world where every interaction is facilitated through technology. Using Artificial Intelligence to interrogate performativity, beauty and race through the construction of our online selves, the piece explores what identity means in the hyper commercialised online world.

Is Anybody There? is a hybrid digital/live performance exploring themes of race, sexuality and commercialism in the age of digital connection. The project explores the creative potential of audience interaction through digital means. Using rollerdance to tell the story of connection, the audience will be invited to interact with the performers through Twitch throughout the short performance.

Technology

Is Anybody There? links Twitch to robotic costume. Set design and audio design by AI helpers.

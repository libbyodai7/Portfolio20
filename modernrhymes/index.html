Hello, my name is Georgie Lock, I am the founder of Modern Rhymes and all I want to do is help others achieve happiness.

After some… unpleasantness I suffered years ago, which by the way was not my fault – AT ALL. I decided to start Modern Rhymes all by myself, I’m a very strong person, and others need my strength to help them get through difficult times.

Some people (not me) can’t get over their problems unless they have someone to talk to, someone to confide in, and someone they trust. At first I thought I would hire a professional, someone who’s studied the human mind for decades. But I decided it wasn’t necessary, and that I can do just as good a job as they can (I’m an excellent judge of character).

So far it’s been a huge success, it didn’t take long for me to earn the trust and respect of all my patients, and I’ve showed them all a road to a better life. I guess you might say I’m the coach driver, safely transporting them to a better place, but I don’t do it for money, I do it for joy.

If you would like to join the support group, you can get in touch with me through the website. If your problem doesn’t need full time counselling, then you can ask anonymously through the website as well, I promise my wisdom will be nothing but beneficial to you.

Remember, with all of your doubts and all of your fears, only Georgie can dry your tears.
